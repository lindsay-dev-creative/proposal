Where you are

Thank you for taking the time to walk me through where Dynamic Cleaning Service is headed. You and your wife are relaunching with a clear focus on commercial work, you are bonded and insured, which sets you apart from most of the field, and you have real contracts in your sights, starting with the Clemson dormitory bid.

Your brochures from True Color are almost ready, and the QR code on them points to a website that does not exist yet. This proposal lays out a site built to support your commercial growth, and structured so the homepage is live before those brochures start circulating.

What this website needs to do

  • Give Dynamic Cleaning Service a professional online presence that earns trust with commercial clients and contract decision makers.
  • Send everyone who scans the brochure QR code to a clear, credible site where they can see what you do and reach out.
  • Turn interest into booked estimates through a contact and booking form tied to your calendar, so a request becomes a scheduled visit without the back and forth.
  • Lead with the things that win commercial work: bonded and insured, free estimates, free sample cleaning, and your track record.
  • Give you a foundation you can grow into as reviews, job photos, and hiring information come in.

Scope of work

A four page custom website, written and designed from the ground up. No template look, no filler.

Home

Your first impression

Clear headline and tagline, a quick look at what you offer, the reasons to call you over anyone else, a testimonials section, and an obvious way to request a free estimate.

About

Your story and credibility

The relaunch, your years in the business, the fact that you are bonded and insured, the areas you serve across the Greenville Upstate, and your past work. We can feature your Century 21 reference with your approval.

Services

What you do, laid out plainly

Commercial cleaning, new construction cleaning, daily, weekly, or monthly schedules, and the specialty work you can bring in such as floor buffing and waxing. Free estimate and free sample cleaning noted clearly.

Contact

The page that does the work

A booking form for prospects to request a free estimate and pick a time, your contact details, and your service area.

Included with the build

  • Copywriting for all four pages, written in your voice.
  • A booking form with calendar integration, so prospects can request a free estimate and choose a time that works for both of you.
  • Email automation that confirms each request and keeps you from chasing every lead by hand.
  • Iconography pulled from your brochure so the site and print materials feel like one brand.
  • A new email domain, added and connected, so you have a professional business email that matches your website.

The site is built on Framer. I handle the build, maintenance, gallery uploads, and any bugs, so you can stay focused on running the business.

Project process

Here is how we will work together from start to finish.

  1. Sitemap and wireframes

    I start with the sitemap and wireframes. Think of this as the blueprint for your website. Just as a home builder needs blueprints to build a house the right way, a web designer needs a sitemap and wireframes to plan where everything goes before any design begins.

  2. Homepage design and review

    Next I design the homepage. Before I move into development, we set up a video call to review it together so we are aligned on the direction before the build begins.

  3. Homepage development

    I develop the homepage and connect your domain to the new site by June 30. That gives us a couple of days to test everything before your July 2 deadline.

  4. Remaining pages and review

    I design the remaining pages. Before they go live, we have a second video call to walk through your feedback, and I make any revisions from there.

  5. Development, testing, and launch

    I develop the remaining pages and test everything thoroughly to make sure the site runs smoothly. Then we launch. The current estimated completion date is August 28, 2026. This can shift depending on when we are able to schedule our review calls and how quickly we exchange feedback.
