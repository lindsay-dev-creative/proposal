Previous call recap

You asked to see what this would look like as a landing page only, so this version is scoped to one focused page rather than the full multi page site. It gives the brochure QR code a real, credible home and a clear way for prospects to reach you, with room to grow into a larger site later.

What this landing page needs to do

  • Give Dynamic Cleaning Services a professional online presence that earns trust with commercial clients and contract decision makers.
  • Send everyone who scans the brochure QR code to a clear, credible page where they can see what you do and reach out.
  • Turn interest into booked estimates through your automated booking form, so a request becomes a scheduled visit without the back and forth.
  • Lead with the things that win commercial work: bonded and insured, free estimates, free sample cleaning, and your track record.
  • Give you a foundation you can grow into a full site as reviews, job photos, and hiring information come in.

Scope of work

A single custom landing page, written and designed from the ground up. No template look, no filler. Everything a prospect needs on one focused, scrollable page.
Section 01

Services

Commercial cleaning, new construction cleaning, daily, weekly, or monthly schedules, and the specialty work you can bring in such as floor buffing and waxing. Free estimate and free sample cleaning noted clearly.

Section 02

Mission and about

Your mission and the story behind Dynamic Cleaning Services. The relaunch, your years in the business, the fact that you are bonded and insured, and the areas you serve across the Greenville Upstate.

Section 03

Imagery

Clean, professional imagery, that I source and place for you, so the page looks polished and on brand from day one without waiting on a photo shoot.

Section 04

Footer

An automated booking form for prospects to request a free estimate and pick a time, your contact details, your service area, and links to your social profiles if you have them.

Included with the build

  • Imagery sourced and placed for you, so the page looks polished and on brand without waiting on a photo shoot.
  • A booking form in the footer with calendar integration, so prospects can request a free estimate and choose a time that works for both of you.
  • Email automation that confirms each request and keeps you from chasing every lead by hand.
  • Social links in the footer for any profiles you have.
  • Iconography pulled from your brochure so the page and your print materials feel like one brand.
The page is built on Framer. I handle the build, maintenance, image updates, and any bugs, so you can stay focused on running the business.

Copywriting for the full landing page, written in your voice, is available as an add-on at $0.50 per word.
