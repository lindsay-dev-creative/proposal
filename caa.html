Where CAA is now

  • South Carolina’s largest auto auction, running on weekly sales, recurring events, and steady promos
  • Marketing speaks to dealers, not the public, so design has to be fast, consistent, and professional
  • Katie leads brand, campaigns, and approvals. Zach sets direction. Isaiah runs video and social visuals
  • Cassie retires at the end of July, and the priority is coverage without a gap
  • Starting while she’s still here lets me learn your systems and take over her files cleanly

Where CAA is headed

  • The current materials no longer match the quality of the operation
  • A modern, bolder look, with updated brand guidelines to support it
  • Elevated booklets and marketing materials give your sales team a stronger tool for winning new dealers

Why we’re a good fit

So much of your marketing runs on a rhythm: weekly sales, quarterly promotions and events, and the occasional trade show. That kind of recurring, deadline-driven work is exactly what I enjoy and am set up to handle well. What you can expect:

  • A smooth handoff. I'll review Cassie's templates and files and coordinate with her directly so nothing gets lost in the transition.
  • A modern, bold direction. That fresher, bolder look is where CAA is headed, and it's what I'd carry across your promos, apparel, and event materials.
  • Adobe-based, print-ready delivery. Working with Adobe and print vendors for 10+ years, I prepare clean, print-ready files, with the option to coordinate directly with your vendor.
