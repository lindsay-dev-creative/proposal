The Projects

01

Shopify Website Build

Luxury Personal Care Brand · Shopify

Your client’s brand is positioned as an heirloom: high-end personal care objects made from luxe materials, meant to be kept rather than replaced. The brand identity and guidelines are already in place. The site needs to finally honor them, with the moody, saturated, luxe direction the brand was always intended to carry rather than the lighter look it ended up with.

The site is currently migrating from Squarespace to Shopify, and La Fleur Society will receive it once that transition is complete. Because the exact condition of the migrated theme will not be known until then, the work below is scoped as a flexible range. It may land closer to refined page-level styling, or closer to a near rebuild of key templates. I will confirm the precise hours after a full review of the migrated store.

What This Project Covers

  • Brand-true visual direction. Apply the existing brand guidelines faithfully across the store, leaning into the deep green, navy, linen, oxblood, and cognac leather palette and the saturated, editorial feel the brand was built for.
  • Shop by ritual and by material. Build the browsing experience around the way the brand wants to be explored, by ritual (hair, teeth, and more) and by material, with room for curated sets.
  • Founder-forward storytelling. Center the founder as the face of the brand. Co-founder presence stays in descriptions and social only, kept off the primary pages as directed.
  • Refined product and collection templates. Product, collection, and landing templates styled to luxury standard, with considered typography, spacing, and imagery treatment.
  • Built to grow. Structure the store so future additions, such as personalization, can be layered in without a rebuild.
  • Designer and client directions side by side. Where it helps the decision, I will show the client-preferred direction next to my recommended direction, so the choice is clear and informed.

Dependency: This project begins once Jody or Margaux confirms the client site has finished migrating from Squarespace to Shopify. I will then audit the migrated store and provide a confirmed hour estimate and timeline before any work begins.

02

Event Landing Page & RSVP System

Influencer Event · Outreach to RSVP

For the upcoming influencer event, the goal is to gauge interest and collect mailing addresses before physical invitations go out. I will build a fast, on-brand flow that captures every response cleanly and lands it where your team can act on it.

The Flow

  • Personalized outreach email. Sent from a recognized La Fleur Society team member so recipients immediately know the sender, with a simple yes or no interest prompt.
  • Landing page for contact details. A quick, elegant page that collects each guest’s mailing address and contact information for the invitation send.
  • Automatic Google Sheets integration. Every submission feeds directly into a master Google Sheet in real time, so the guest list builds itself with no manual entry.
  • RSVP-ready. The same approach extends to RSVP collection once invitations go out, mirroring the QR-to-RSVP flow used on past events.
  • Kept separate from the client website. The landing page lives on its own, independent of the client store, and can be turned around quickly.
Fast to launch

The core landing page is a roughly one to two hour build, so outreach can begin without delay.

Zero manual tracking

Responses and addresses flow straight into a single shared sheet, ready for the invitation mailing.
